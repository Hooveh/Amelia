Amelia Corbin

Adoptive daughter of Angelo Corbin, by the young age of 5 could Amelia already claim to know both what it's like to starve in the streets, and what it's like to be a noble in high society.

With a cautious but caring nature, her eyes give her strange troubles, relying on special glasses to give her proper sight.

Over the years she's become more mature and proactive. What was once a shell has made her a beautiful butterfly.
